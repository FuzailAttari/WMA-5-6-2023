For the Fulfilment of Needs

It has been mentioned in a Hadis that the Merciful Prophet has stated
I know one such Ayah that if people were to become practising on it, their needs would be fulfilled
He then recited the following Ayah. For repaying debts and increasing sustenance, abundant recitation of this Ayah is proven beneficial

وَ مَنْ يَتَّقِ اللَّهَ يَجْعَلْ لَّهُ مَخْرَجًا ويَرْزُقْهُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَحْتَسِبُ وَمَن يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللهِ فَهُوَ حَسْبُهُ إِنَّ اللَّهَ بَالِغُ أَمْرِهِ قَدْ جَعَلَ اللَّهُ لِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدْرًا
(Part 28, Surah at-talaq, verses 2, 3) (Madani Panj Surah, p. 232)
